How to Unblock Action Block on Facebook

Steps to Follow:

  1. Open the Report a Problem page on Facebook.
  2. Select the appropriate category from the available options.
  3. Provide a brief but clear description of the issue you're facing.
  4. Attach relevant screenshots to support your request.
  5. Submit the form and monitor your email for updates from Facebook.
