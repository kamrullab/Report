Restricted Monetization Issue

Guide to Resolve Restricted Monetization

Overview: Your Facebook page may encounter a restricted monetization status due to policy violations. Follow the steps below to resolve this issue.

Steps to Resolve:

  1. Check Alerts: Go to Support Inbox and check for any updates under Your Alerts.
  2. Review Violations: Carefully read and address the flagged content or videos.
  3. Make Corrections: Modify or remove content as required by Facebook’s policy.
  4. Wait for Review: It may take 5-7 business days for Facebook to review your updates.
  5. Submit a Report: If unresolved, submit a report using Facebook’s Report a Problem tool.
