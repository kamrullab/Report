Step-by-Step Guide to Submit Your Report

  1. Click the "Report Profile Issues" link above.
  2. Log in to your Facebook account if required.
  3. Attach relevant screenshots and documents.
  4. Select the issue type (e.g., Profile Restriction, Login Issue).
  5. Click Submit to finalize your report.
